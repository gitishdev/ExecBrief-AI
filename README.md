# ExecBrief AI

**Distill hours of video into minutes of insight.**

ExecBrief AI is a powerful productivity tool designed to instantly transform long-form YouTube videos into concise, highly actionable, one-page executive summaries. 

## What is this tool for?
With the explosion of podcasts, tech talks, and long-form educational content on YouTube, it has become impossible to watch everything. ExecBrief AI solves this problem by using advanced AI to analyze a video and extract the signal from the noise. 

For any given YouTube URL, the tool generates a structured brief containing:
*   **Metadata:** Video Title, Channel Name, Upload Date, and Duration.
*   **Speakers:** Background information on the hosts and guests.
*   **Executive Summary:** A high-level overview of the video's core premise.
*   **Key Takeaways:** The top strategic insights and advice.
*   **Actionable Insights:** Specific, granular, step-by-step methods, configurations, or frameworks mentioned in the video that you can immediately implement.

## Who can benefit from using it?
ExecBrief AI is built for anyone whose time is their most valuable asset:
*   **Busy Executives & Leaders:** Quickly grasp the strategic takeaways of industry podcasts, keynotes, or interviews without spending 2 hours watching them.
*   **Product Managers & Strategists:** Extract actionable frameworks and tactical details from product teardowns and expert panels.
*   **Developers & Engineers:** Pull specific configurations, architectural decisions, and coding methodologies from long technical talks.
*   **Researchers & Students:** Rapidly synthesize educational content and save it for future reference.

## Key Features
*   **AI-Powered Synthesis:** Leverages Google's Gemini 3.1 Pro model to deeply analyze video content and search the web for context.
*   **Optimized PDF Export:** Download your summaries as crisp, beautifully formatted PDFs. The export engine is highly optimized, producing files under 1MB for easy sharing.
*   **Serverless Shareable Links:** Share your generated summaries instantly. The app compresses the entire summary into a clean, URL-safe token, allowing others to view your brief without needing a backend database or requiring them to re-run the AI generation.
*   **Local History:** Automatically caches your generated summaries in your browser's local storage, providing a clean History dashboard to revisit past insights instantly.

## How to use
1. Paste up to 3 YouTube URLs into the input fields.
2. Click **Generate Synthesis**.
3. Read the generated brief, download it as a PDF, or copy a shareable link to send to your team.
